About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my personal website. Here you will find some information about me and a link to my portfolio. I created this website to showcase some of my web development skills and as place to keep my portfolio updated.

I have lived in the Puget Sound region my entire life being born in Federal Way and raised in Woodinville. I currently live in Queen Anne with my girlfriend, Tessa, and dog, Stewart. I graduated from Western Washington University with a BA in Business Administration concentration in Finance and a minor in Economics. I am currently enrolled in UW's Coding Bootcamp to expand my skills and learn web development and to break into the technology field. Click on the portfolio tab at the top to view some of the projects I have worked on in this class.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy taking my dog for walks along the Ship Canal Trail on beautiful Seattle summer days, trying new beers from the many different breweries throughout the city, and listening to music and attending live shows. I am an avid sports fan and love attending pretty much any sporting event, but I mostly follow the NFL and NBA. I am also a fantasy football enthusiast and have been playing since I was in middle school.
